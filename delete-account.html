Account Deletion Request

We understand that you may want to delete your ShelfWise account and associated data. This page explains how to request account deletion and what data will be removed or retained.

Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your inventory data, files, and app preferences will be permanently deleted. Please make sure to export any important data before proceeding.

How to Request Account Deletion

1

Send Email Request

Send an email to our support team with your deletion request. Include your registered email address in the message.

2

Identity Verification

We will verify your identity to ensure the request is legitimate and protect your account security.

3

Confirmation

You will receive a confirmation email once your account deletion request has been processed.

4

Complete Deletion

Your account and associated data will be permanently deleted within 30 days of request confirmation.

Data Retention Period

Most personal data will be deleted immediately upon request processing. However, some data may be retained for up to 90 days for the following reasons:

  • Legal compliance and regulatory requirements
  • Fraud prevention and security purposes
  • Tax and financial record keeping
  • Dispute resolution

After this retention period, all remaining data will be permanently and irreversibly deleted from our systems.

Alternative: Selective Data Deletion

If you don't want to delete your entire account but would like to remove specific data, you can:

  • Delete individual products from your inventory
  • Remove uploaded files through the app
  • Clear your preferences in Settings
  • Contact us for assistance with partial data removal

Questions or Concerns?

If you have any questions about our data deletion process or need assistance, please don't hesitate to contact our support team. We're here to help ensure your privacy rights are respected.
